There are 5 line modeling states:

  • 0 known segments are found, headSz and tailSegSz are unknown (only for initialization)
  • 1 known segment is found, headSz and tailSegSz are unknown
  • 1 known segment is found, only tailSegSz is known
  • 2 known segments are found, headSz and tailSegSz are unknown, tailSz is unknown
  • 2 known segments are found, headSz and tailSegSz are unknown, tailSz is known

After the last state, tailSz will be the new known segment. As the raster line is modeled, it will loop through 4 states. tailSz will equal either headSz or tailSegSz. tailSz plus or minus priorTailSegSz will equal either headSz or tailSegSz, whichever one has not been assigned.
