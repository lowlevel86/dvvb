trunOffset -- truncated offset
iniSeg -- initial segment
secSeg -- second segment

if iniSeg == secSeg+1 then

   iniSeg is the size of secSeg+1


if iniSeg == secSeg then

   iniSeg is the size of secSeg or secSeg+1


if iniSeg < secSeg then

   iniSeg is the size of secSeg-1 or secSeg or secSeg+1
   account trunOffset and ignore
